# Recipe for makaronilaatikko (pasta bake)

## Ingredients:
 - 400g uncooked pasta
 - 400g uncooked mincemeat
 - 1/2 tsp salt
 - 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
 - 1 l milk
 - 3 eggs

 ## Instructions:

 1. Heat oven to 175 degrees Celsius
 2. Heat c. 1/2 l of water to boil
 3. Add pasta to water
 4. Cook pasta (until al dente, c. 6 minutes)
 5. Cook mincemeat
 6. Add mincemeat to pasta and mix
 7. Separately, add the milk to a bowl
 8. Add the eggs one by one to the bowl and mix well
 9. Add salt and pepper to milk and eggs and mix well
 10. Grease oven dish with butter or margarine
 11. Add pasta and mincemeat mixture to oven dish
 12. Pour egg-milk mixture over pasta-mincemeat
 13. Cook in oven for c. 1 hour
